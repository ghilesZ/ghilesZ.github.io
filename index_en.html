Who am I?

My name is Ghiles Ziat and i am a phd student since the 1st January 2016, in the APR team of the LIP6 laboratory. My advisors are: Antoine Miné et Charlotte Truchet.

Research

My research topic is the study of combinations between abstract interpretation and constraint programming. The aim of my thesis is to propose hybrid techniques from these two domains, in the context of problem solving and static analysis. My work takes place within the coverif anr.
